Solar Eclipses

A solar eclipse occurs when the moon gets between Earth and the sun, and casts a shadow over Earth. A solar eclipse can only take place at the phase of new moon, when the moon passes directly between the sun and Earth and its shadows fall upon Earth's surface.

Lunar Eclipses

Lunar Eclipses

A lunar eclipse is an astronomical phenomenon. It happens when the moon passes through the shadow of the Earth which can only occur during a full moon. The Earth casts a shadow on the moon and makes it look how it does.
